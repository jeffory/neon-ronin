# Assets

**Art direction:** Dark cyberpunk Tokyo at night. Narrow alleyways flanked by tall buildings with glowing neon signs in Japanese characters. Dominant palette: vivid pink, magenta, cyan, and purple neon against dark steel and concrete. Wet asphalt streets reflecting neon light. Dense atmospheric haze with volumetric glow. Futuristic but gritty — exposed wiring, vending machines, cluttered signage. Weapons have a sleek sci-fi aesthetic with cyan/teal accents and subtle emissive details. HUD is minimal and clean with a tech/holographic feel.
